What we do

OUR SERVICES

WeCrest provides software tools and services to intellectual property (IP) law firms around the world. Our tools and services allow law firms to maximize the cost-efficiency of their marketing strategy and develop their business with trackable and quantifiable results.

We take advantage of big data, analytics, specialist knowledge of the IP field, cloud technology, expertise in software development and programming, and a keen eye for design and details to create solutions that simplify and improve the lives of WeCrest members, customers, and partners.

Our identity

The concept behind the ‘Summit’ logo for WeCrest was inspired by the action implied by the company’s name and objectives. To reach the top or summit of something is to ‘crest’ said object.

The peak of the ‘Summit’ logo breaks through the bounds of the circular border, going above and beyond, displaying WeCrest’s dedication to exemplary service. The steps in elevation are also a visual representation of the journey on which WeCrest guides its users through to their final goal.
